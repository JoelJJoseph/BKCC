Why Choose Black Knight Chess Center?

At BKCC, we nurture future champions with expert coaching, a robust curriculum, and opportunities to grow both intellectually and competitively. Here's what we offer:

Flexible Chess Coaching

Learn from FIDE-rated coaches with online and offline classes designed to fit your schedule. Train from home for maximum convenience.

Comprehensive Curriculum

Access a structured chess syllabus and premium study materials in both digital and print formats to boost your learning.

Quarterly Skill Assessments

Track your progress with quarterly evaluations to refine your skills and strategies.

Competitive Chess Tournaments

Compete in monthly online tournaments and quarterly in-person tournaments to sharpen your chess abilities.

Pioneering Chess

Coaching for Kids

At BKCC , we redefine the game of chess as a powerful tool for personal and intellectual growth. Our online chess coaching inspires children to develop essential 21st-century skills such as critical thinking, problem-solving, and strategic planning. With our innovative in-house technology and seamless integration with a global learning platform, we connect young minds to a vibrant community of chess enthusiasts worldwide. Under the guidance of our patient mentors, children engage in a dynamic learning environment that promotes meaningful screen time. From interactive lessons to live tournaments, we provide the flexibility, structure, and support children need to excel—all from the comfort and safety of home. At BKCC, we cultivate a lifelong love for chess and the skills to succeed in any arena.
